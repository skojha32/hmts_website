About HMTS

Logistics is the lifeline of any company and HMTS understands the need for hassle free and quality delivery services. Started in 1994, HMTS was founded realizing the requirement for a transport service that valued being secure and reliable. Over the years, we have built a well-trusted logistics service that is flexible, secure and focused on the needs of customers.

Our Founder

Mr. Hari Kant Ojha

Founded in 1994 by Mr. Hari Kant Ojha, HMTS began with a vision to provide reliable and efficient transportation services to businesses in Bangalore. Starting with a single tempo, Mr. Hari Kant Ojha built HMTS on the foundations of trust, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

With over five decades of experience in the logistics industry, his leadership and dedication have transformed HMTS into one of the most trusted names in transportation services. His values of integrity, punctuality, and commitment to quality continue to guide our operations today.

"Success in logistics is built on trust, reliability, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction."
